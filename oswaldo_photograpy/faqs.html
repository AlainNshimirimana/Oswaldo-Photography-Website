FAQs

Q: What is the price?
The price will depend on the theme that you’re looking for and the location where you would like to meet up, plus the amount of photos you would like to edit. You can contact me for more information.
Q: Are you able to meet up anywhere when we can do a photoshoot?
yes! but of course, there will be some extra fee depending on the location (how far the destination) and the area where you’re planning to meet up.
Q: Do you charge extra if I'm bringing someone along with but for only wanting one picture with the person?
First picture is free with the person but having more than one would be an extra fee.
Q: Do you personally edit the pictures or do I have to go somewhere and get them to edit?
I edit them. you get 2 free if you’re a brand you client! I charge only by the number of images you like me to edit. The rest would be given in RAW images.
Q: Do you take PayPal/Venmo as a payment?
it's 2019 right? so yes I do but no Bitcoin..…maybe.
Q: How long would it take to receive the Pictures?
Depends on the number of images I need to edit and the amount of client I have. Usually takes me between 3-4 business days.
